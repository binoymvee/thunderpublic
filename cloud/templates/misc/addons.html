What is an Add-ons?

Thunder add-ons allow custom capabilities that may not be included with Thunder by default.


Are there any limitations?

Thunder supports storage add-ons which can only be only be consumed by clouds created after the add-on was uploaded.


How do I use an active plugin?

Thunder add-ons are available immediately following upload and consumable by all deployments.
